Who we are?

At SVIET (Swami Vivekanand Institute of Engineering & Technology), our Grievance Cell serves as a vital avenue for students, faculty, and staff to address any concerns or grievances they may encounter during their academic or professional journey. Led by a dedicated team of professionals, the Grievance Cell is committed to providing a supportive and confidential environment where individuals feel comfortable expressing their issues. Whether it's related to academics, infrastructure, or personal matters, we prioritize listening attentively to all concerns and working towards equitable resolutions. Upholding principles of fairness, transparency, and accountability, SVIET's Grievance Cell plays a crucial role in fostering a positive and conducive learning and working environment for everyone within our institution.
