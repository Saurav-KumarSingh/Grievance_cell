Who we are?

At SVIET, we pride ourselves on being a warm and welcoming academic community located right in the heart of SVIET. Our college is a place where everyone feels included, valued, and supported. We strive to create a friendly environment where students can thrive both academically and personally. Whether you're pursuing your passion in engineering, technology, or any other field, we're here to help you succeed. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing quality education and guidance to every student. We believe in nurturing talent and helping each individual reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To become one of the most preferred learning places as a leading global technical institute that aims to bring a revolutionary change in education to create luminaries and innovators, and also propagate new knowledge for society and industry. The COVID-19 pandemic has significantly disrupted the higher education sector. In this challenging situation, our vision is to provide students a learning environment at their home with regular online classes.
